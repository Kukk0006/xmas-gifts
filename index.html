In the jars

Spicy Northern Soup Mix

Spice up your mealtime with the Spicy Northern Soup Mix! Packed with a diverse bean medley—from lima and kidney beans to lentils and garbanzos—and dried veggies like jalapeños and bell peppers, this mix is a flavorful kick to your soup bowl.

Directions for Use:
For a single serving: Add 1/4 cup (44 g) of soup mix to 1 1/4 cup (310 ml) of liquid (water, chicken, vegetable or beef broth). Bring to a boil for 5 minutes, then a strong simmer, covered for 60 minutes. Season to taste.

Wild Rice Vegetable Soup Mix

For a comforting and hearty option, try the Wild Rice Vegetable Soup Mix. Loaded with wild rice, barley, split peas, and a blend of dehydrated vegetables including carrots, onions, and peppers, it's a wholesome bowlful of goodness that'll warm you up from the inside out.

Directions for Use:
For small serving of 1-2 people use 1/4 cup (42g) of wild rice vegetable soup mix and 2 cups (500ml) of broth. Combine wild rice vegetable soup mix with pre-made or homemade beef, chicken or vegetable broth. Bring to a boil and simmer for 45-60 minutes. Add extra water to reach desired thickness.
